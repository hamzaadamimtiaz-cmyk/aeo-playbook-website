Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Threads and Trade LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use aeoplaybook.store (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase a Product or interact with our Service, we may collect:

  • Contact information: Your name and email address, provided at checkout
  • Payment information: Processed by our payment provider (Stripe and/or Lemon Squeezy). We do not store your full payment card details on our servers.
  • Transaction details: Order ID, purchase date, product purchased, amount paid
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring URLs (collected automatically via standard web analytics)
  • Marketing data: UTM parameters from advertising sources, when applicable

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver the Product you purchased
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, file delivery, updates to the Product)
  • Provide customer support
  • Send occasional updates about new editions of the Product or related offerings, if you've opted in
  • Improve our Service and understand how customers find and use it
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We share information with service providers who help us operate the Service:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, Lemon Squeezy): to process transactions
  • Email delivery services: to send Product files and updates
  • Analytics providers (Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, or similar): to understand how visitors interact with our Service
  • Hosting providers: to deliver our website

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our Service uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Analytics and performance measurement
  • Advertising and conversion tracking (Meta Pixel)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may limit some functionality.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide ongoing access to the Product and updates
  • Comply with legal, accounting, or reporting requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting aeoplaybook@protonmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact aeoplaybook@protonmail.com.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and trusted third-party processors. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. You use the Service at your own risk.

8. International Users

Our Service is operated from the United States. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your home country.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the Service.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to aeoplaybook@protonmail.com
